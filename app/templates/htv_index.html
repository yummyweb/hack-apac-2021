EmotionSwitch

Suspecting yourself on mental issues? Dont worry we got you, Use our machine learning powered tool to get a detalied report on your emotions, behaviour and mood-changes and get suggestions too!!

Start A SessionGithub

Features:

Session

Just start a session! EmotionSwitch takes your webcam feed which is then fed to a powerful machine learning model that generates your report when you end the current session

Track

EmotionSwitch's Machine-learning model is trained to track realtime changes. With very low latency it detects mood swing or emotion changes each second which is displayed by a graph

Report

After you end a session, EmotionSwitch generates a detailed report about your emotional behaviour. It also suggests you things like a song according to your dominant mood. You can also download a shareable report
